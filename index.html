Floor Manifest
Made in USA · SmartStrength

Prime Fitness

Prime built its name on biomechanics instead of brute steel. Every machine carries their patented SmartStrength system, which lets you shift the peak resistance to the lengthened, mid, or shortened part of the range. You load the muscle the way it actually produces force, from origin to insertion, and you feel it change rep to rep. The bearings and cables track so cleanly that heavy still feels smooth. It is premium American iron, and most commercial gyms never get near it.

  • Patented SmartStrength variable resistance
  • Load the lengthened, mid or shortened range
  • Made in the USA, frame to weight stack
  • Smooth tracking under heavy load
Since 1970 · The original cam

Nautilus

Nautilus is where machine training started. In 1970 Arthur Jones cut the shell-shaped cam that gave resistance a curve, matching the load to your strength through the full range instead of fighting it. Heaviest where you are strong, lighter where you are exposed, kinder on the joints and harder on the muscle. The pieces on our floor carry that same engineering. This is heritage iron that still trains better than most machines built today.

  • The original shell cam, proven since 1970
  • Resistance matched to your strength curve
  • Full range tension, joint-friendly load
  • Heritage engineering, built to outlast trends
South Korea · M Torture

Newtech

Newtech is the South Korean brand the Mr. Olympia stage runs on. The M Torture plate-loaded line is built around the big compound lifts and the large muscle groups, with grip and foot positions that adjust to any frame so the strength line stays honest whether you are short or tall. Heavy, dead smooth, and finished in top-tier materials. It has earned a hard following with serious bodybuilders, and you feel why the moment you load the first plate.

  • M Torture plate-loaded series
  • Built around heavy compound movements
  • Grip and foot positions for any frame
  • Mr. Olympia title equipment

Philosophy

Three rules.
No exceptions.

Rule 01

Equipment First

The money goes into the iron, not the lobby. Every machine here was picked because it trains the muscle properly. No filler stations, no broken cables, no cheap substitutes to save a few euro.

Rule 02

No Noise

No group classes. No circuits. No timed buzzers, no synced playlists. You come here to put in the work, and that is the only thing this place is built for.

Rule 03

Earned Access

Membership is limited and kept that way on purpose. A capped roster means open equipment, room to move, and a floor full of people who take it as seriously as you do.
