Philosophy

Three rules.
No exceptions.

Equipment First

The money goes into the iron, not the lobby. Every machine here was picked because it trains the muscle properly. No filler stations, no broken cables, no cheap substitutes to save a few euro.

No Noise

No group classes. No circuits. No timed buzzers, no synced playlists. You come here to put in the work, and that is the only thing this place is built for.

Earned Access

Membership is limited and kept that way on purpose. A capped roster means open equipment, room to move, and a floor full of people who take it as seriously as you do.
